About Me

Martial Arts Instructor & Practitioner

I am Umer Khan, a dedicated martial artist with over 10 years of experience in Ninjutsu and Judo. With a Black Belt in Ninjutsu and a Blue Belt in Judo, I am passionate about teaching others the art of self-defense, discipline, and personal growth through martial arts.

My teaching philosophy centers around building a strong foundation of technical skills while emphasizing the mental aspects that make martial arts a way of life - focus, respect, perseverance, and balance.
